The Path to SOC Analyst

Becoming a SOC analyst isn't about collecting certifications or watching tutorials. It's about building practical skills daily, understanding both attack and defense, and creating real infrastructure.

The path requires consistent execution. Not motivation. Not inspiration. Discipline. Show up every day, even when you don't feel like it. Especially when you don't feel like it.

"That's the Zaki way."

This journey means building over consuming. Create tools. Deploy infrastructure. Document the process. Don't just take coursesâ€”apply what you learn immediately in practical projects that demonstrate real capability.

Understanding both offensive and defensive security is critical. You can't defend what you don't understand. Red team knowledge makes you a better blue team operator. Study attacks to build better defenses.

The roadmap: Hands-on labs (TryHackMe, HackTheBox), custom tool development (Python, Bash), production infrastructure (VPS, DNS, SSL), and continuous documentation (blogs, portfolios, GitHub).
