The Path to SOC Analyst

Becoming a SOC analyst isn't about collecting certifications or watching tutorials. It's about building practical skills daily, understanding both attack and defense, and creating real infrastructure.

The path requires consistent execution. Not motivation. Not inspiration. Discipline. Show up every day, even when you don't feel like it. Especially when you don't feel like it.

"That's the Zaki way."
